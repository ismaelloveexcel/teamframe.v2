# Pricing and Packages

## Positioning Note

This is not SaaS pricing. It is not per-seat pricing. It is not consultancy day-rate pricing.

It is a **managed service retainer** — a fixed monthly fee for a defined scope of HR operational work, supported by TeamFrame as the internal system.

Buyers understand projects and retainers. They do not need to understand software subscriptions to say yes.

---

## Package 1 — System Only

**£900 / month**

### What is included

- TeamFrame maintained and kept current (org structure, assignments, positions)
- Monthly org health summary delivered in writing to the founder (1 page maximum)
  - What changed this month
  - What gaps currently exist
  - What was closed or resolved
- Async support with 48-hour response SLA
- No fixed working days

### Who this is for

Stable companies with low hiring velocity. Founder wants the system accurate and a regular view of org health, but does not need hands-on HR work every month.

### Capacity

Up to 10 clients at this tier for a solo operator.

---

## Package 2 — Two Days Per Month

**£1,750 / month**

### What is included

- Everything in Package 1
- 2 structured working days per month (defined as 8 pre-agreed work blocks — see scope-and-boundaries.md for definition)
- Typical work in these blocks:
  - Job description creation or update (up to 3 roles per block)
  - New hire setup (role, assignment, onboarding checklist)
  - Role change or transfer processing
  - Compliance document tracking update
  - Org structure change (reporting line, department change)

### Who this is for

Actively hiring companies. Adding 1–2 people per month. Founder wants structured HR support without a full-time hire.

### Capacity

Up to 8 clients at this tier for a solo operator.

---

## Package 3 — One Day Per Week

**£3,000 / month**

### What is included

- Everything in Package 2
- 4 structured working days per month (defined as weekly 4-hour embedded HR operations blocks — see scope-and-boundaries.md)
- Full fractional HR presence:
  - Active hiring support (JDs, role approvals, hiring manager briefings)
  - Policy creation and updates
  - Team query handling (via async channel within defined windows)
  - Restructuring support
  - Monthly org health report expanded to include action items

### Who this is for

Fast-growing companies (35–60 employees), hiring frequently, no internal HR person, founder or COO handling people decisions personally and running out of capacity.

### Capacity

Maximum 5 clients at this tier. This is a hard cap. Do not exceed it.

## Revenue Model

### Example monthly mix (realistic steady state)

| Package | Clients | Monthly Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| Package 1 (£900) | 3 | £2,700 |
| Package 2 (£1,750) | 4 | £7,000 |
| Package 3 (£3,000) | 3 | £9,000 |
| **Total** | **10** | **£18,700/month** |

### Minimum viable target

4 clients across any mix = £8,000–£10,000/month. This is achievable without a team.

### Onboarding fee (setup)

New clients pay a one-time setup fee before the retainer begins:

| Company size | Setup fee |
|---|---|
| 10–20 employees | £1,500 |
| 20–35 employees | £2,000–£2,500 |
| 35–50 employees | £3,000 |

Setup includes: full org structure built in TeamFrame, all existing roles documented, JDs drafted for key positions, compliance baseline established.

---

## Pricing Principles

1. Never discount the retainer. Adjust scope instead.
2. Never offer unlimited support. Every package has defined boundaries.
3. Setup fee is non-negotiable. It funds the intensive first engagement.
4. Package upgrades happen when the client's hiring velocity outgrows their current tier.
5. Do not add a fourth tier. Complexity beyond three packages creates sales friction without meaningful revenue gain.
