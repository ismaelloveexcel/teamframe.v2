# TeamFrame Managed People-Ops — The Offer

## Positioning Note

This is **not** SaaS pricing, per-seat pricing, or day-rate consultancy. It is a
single **managed-service** offer: a fixed setup fee plus a flat monthly retainer
for a defined scope of people-ops work, with TeamFrame as the system that runs it.

There is **one** offer, not a tiered menu. One offer removes sales friction and
keeps delivery focused. Do not reintroduce tiers.

> TeamFrame is the check-engine light for people operations at founder-led teams
> of 6–25 — it watches contracts, documents, onboarding and offboarding, flags
> every risk, and is set up and run for you in 72 hours.

---

## The Standard Offer

**USD 2,500 setup + USD 2,000 / month — 3-month minimum.**

### Setup (USD 2,500, one-time, 72 hours)

Done-for-you. The client does not configure anything.

- Tenant provisioned and secured (operator-led; no self-serve signup).
- Team and reporting structure loaded (positions, people, assignments).
- Existing contracts and documents loaded and organised.
- Onboarding/offboarding state captured.
- **First Look Report** delivered — what a fresh set of eyes sees that the
  internal team has stopped seeing (see below).

### Monthly (USD 2,000)

- Weekly **risk review** of the account by the operator.
- Emailed **weekly risk summary** of open red/yellow signals *(once shipped —
  V1.5; until then delivered as part of the weekly review)*.
- **Signal triage** — what matters, what to do, in what order.
- **Due-diligence exports** and **finance handoff exports** on request.
- **Monthly review call.**
- Light people-ops advisory within agreed limits.

### Included vs. excluded

**Included:** 72-hour done-for-you setup · tenant provisioning · team & document
loading · weekly risk review · weekly risk summary (when shipped) · signal triage
· due-diligence exports · finance handoff exports · monthly review call · light
people-ops advisory within agreed limits.

**Excluded:** payroll · legal advice · recruiting/ATS · performance reviews ·
compensation benchmarking · EOR · complex custom workflows · integrations
marketplace · self-serve setup.

## The First Look Report

When an org is mapped from the outside for the first time, gaps the team has
stopped seeing become visible. The First Look Report is a one-page written
document delivered at setup capturing:

- What is unclear, undocumented, or broken in the current org.
- Roles with no clear owner.
- Responsibilities that exist only in someone's head.
- Structural and document risks visible from outside.

Most founders say they knew most of it existed — they had just stopped seeing it.
This document alone is typically worth the setup fee.

## Pricing Principles

1. **One offer.** Do not reintroduce tiers; adjust scope, not price.
2. **Never discount the retainer** outside the founding-pilot window.
3. **Setup fee is non-negotiable** — it funds the intensive first 72 hours.
4. **Never offer unlimited support.** Advisory is "within agreed limits."
5. **Cap at 5 clients.** This is a hard cap; it protects delivery quality.
6. **No legal advice, ever.** TeamFrame flags risk; it does not advise on law.
