# Objection Log

Every prospect response goes here. No exceptions.

This is not a document for tracking wins. It is a document for tracking what prospects say when they do not buy — or when they hesitate.

After 10–15 conversations, patterns will emerge. Those patterns should influence positioning and messaging more than any market research.

## Rule

No changes to pricing, packaging, or product are made based on a single objection.

Changes are only made when the same objection appears 3 or more times.
