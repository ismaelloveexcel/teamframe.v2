# TeamFrame Assumptions Register

Last updated: 2026-06-04

## Purpose

This file separates observed problems from proposed solutions so roadmap decisions are tied to evidence quality.

Legend:
- Direct: explicitly supported by available evidence
- Partial: supported indirectly; requires primary validation
- Inferred: product interpretation; not directly evidenced

## Validated assumptions (use for product decisions now)

| Assumption | Evidence type | Confidence | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|
| Organizational visibility degrades during growth | Partial + recurring secondary evidence | Medium-High | Accept |
| Founder-to-manager transition increases ownership ambiguity | Direct (secondary evidence) | Medium-High | Accept |
| Remote/hybrid environments increase dependency on written operating context | Direct | High | Accept |
| Coordination complexity rises faster than process maturity | Partial | Medium | Accept |
| Founders and operators are likely primary buyers | Partial | Medium | Accept |

## Partially validated assumptions (keep, but validate in customer interviews)
