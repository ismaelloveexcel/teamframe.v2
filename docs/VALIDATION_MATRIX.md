## Top assumptions requiring primary validation

These assumptions should be tested in customer interviews before roadmap expansion.

1. Managers need persistent visibility into cross-team ownership boundaries.
2. COO/founder buyers will pay for organizational visibility as a recurring product category.
3. Org Map is used weekly after setup (not only during onboarding/reorg events).
4. Actions should live inside TeamFrame versus remaining external in task systems.
5. Team-linked policies materially improve onboarding speed and role clarity.
6. Buyers prefer org-first terminology (teams, owners, functions) over HR terminology.
7. Accountability gaps are severe enough to justify workflow updates in TeamFrame.
8. Directory usage is frequent as an outcome of org navigation, not as a standalone destination.
9. Ownership clarity demand is better solved by embedded surfaces than by a dedicated registry module.
10. Change-history visibility is useful but not critical during initial adoption.

## Interview evidence template

For each assumption, capture:
- Interview ID / company archetype
- Observed pain statement (verbatim)
- Current workaround
- Frequency / severity
- Evidence outcome: validated / partially validated / invalidated
- Product implication
