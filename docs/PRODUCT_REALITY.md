# TeamFrame Product Reality

Last updated: 2026-06-04

## Core thesis

Scaling startups do not first lose visibility into people; they lose visibility into ownership, structure, accountability, and coordination. People become hard to understand because the organization becomes hard to understand.

## Current product definition

TeamFrame is an organizational operations workspace that helps scaling startups understand how their company actually works.

TeamFrame is not:
- HRIS
- Payroll platform
- ATS
- Performance management system
- Workforce management suite

## Core product sequence

1. Org Map
2. Teams and reporting structure
3. Owners and responsibilities (surfaced through structure and execution)
4. Actions
5. Team-linked policies

Administration and payroll export are supporting capabilities, not strategic product wedges.

## Current module and capability posture

Build with confidence:
- Org Map
- Actions
- Team-linked policies

Structural capability (not a standalone wedge module):
- Team Directory (generated from Org Map -> Teams -> People)

Keep minimal:
- Administration
- Payroll export

Do not build yet:
- Standalone Ownership Registry module (ownership should emerge across teams, roles, actions, and policies)
- Organizational change tracking module
- Approval workflows
- Hiring overlays
- Documentation health scoring
- Regulatory workflows

## Buyer posture

Primary buyers:
- COO
- Founder
- Chief of Staff
- Operations lead

Secondary buyers:
- Head of People
- People Ops

## Scope guardrail

Every feature must answer:

Does this improve organizational visibility, ownership clarity, accountability, or coordination?

If no, reject it.
